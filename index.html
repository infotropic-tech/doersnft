FAQ

  • About Reflect festival

    Reflect is the biggest tech & entrepreneurial festival in Cyprus where the region's doers network, brainstorm and learn from their peers. Conveniently located at the crossroads of the EU, Africa, and the Middle East, it invites the attendees to the bustling seaside city of Limassol every year. The festival earned a reputation as an inventive, fun melting pot of entrepreneurs, investors, and thinkers. Throughout these few days, countless new ideas, projects, and partnerships are born.

  • What is the Doers NFT?

    The Doers NFTs represent your 3 years access to Reflect Festival. There is total of 10 Doers 1.0 NFTs.

    NFT stands for non-fungible token. It is a one-of-a-kind digital token that you can buy or sell. These tokens can represent just about anything, and they use blockchain technology to prove ownership. Sometimes they are associated with a digital image, GIF, or song. Sometimes they can be redeemed for a physical object. Sometimes the owner is entitled to an experience, like a movie or a phone call or access to an event.

  • How can I get a Doers NFT?

    To buy the Doers 1.0 NFT you will need to connect a Web3 wallet such as Metamask to the website and click on the Mint button. Once minted, your NFT will appear in your wallet.

    When you mint an NFT, you begin its existence on the blockchain and become its first owner. Similar to how coins are minted and added into circulation, minting an NFT certifies the token as unique and adds it to the blockchain for the first time.

  • What do I receive with a Doers 1.0 NFT?

    Ownership of a Doers NFT grants you VIP access to Reflect festival and the experiences associated with that NFT for 3 years in a row. Each NFT can be bought, sold, or traded. To ensure we can fulfil your Reflect festival passes and experiences, NFT owners will need to verify their ownership on the redemption page made available each year.
